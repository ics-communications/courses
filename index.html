A Catalogue for Working Adults

Designed for Working Educators

Every course in the catalogue is offered in a format that respects the working life of our students — synchronous evening seminars, blended cohorts, and intensives during school breaks.

  • Evening synchronous sessions (most courses meet 6:00–9:00 PM ET)
  • Most courses are 3 credit hours over a 12-week term
  • Online or blended formats — no relocation required
  • Small cohorts (typically 8–12 students per course)
  • Designed to stack toward the MA, MA-EL, MWS-E, or PhD
Tuition Notes

Team Discounts & Auditor Pricing

School and ministry teams of three or more registering for the same course receive a 15% group discount. Auditors who do not require credit may register at the reduced auditor rate.

For tuition specifics, please contact the Registrar.
