Who We Are

An interdisciplinary graduate school where the gospel's message of renewal guides our learning and research

For over fifty years we've formed scholars, educators, pastors, and public leaders through programs that bring together theology, philosophy, education, and culture—focusing on the intersection of reformational philosophy and contemporary scholarship and society.

What Makes ICS Distinctive

✦

Spacious & Inclusive

Intellectual hospitality across traditions. A home for those seeking faith with room to question, explore, and grow.

◈

Scholarly & Practical

Academic excellence joined with spiritual formation and lived public impact. Scholarship that shapes the world.

⟡

Three Streams of Study

I

Philosophical Apprenticeship

How can philosophy help engage my faith with the world honestly and deeply?

Rigorous graduate training in the philosophical foundations of the disciplines, shaped by the reformational tradition and aimed at bringing faith and scholarship into transformative dialogue.

  • PhD Doctorate in Philosophy
  • MA Master of Arts in Philosophy

II

Educational Leadership

How can I renew my teaching so my students can connect faith, learning, and life?

Equipping Christian educators and school leaders with worldview-conscious, philosophically grounded approaches to teaching, curriculum, and formation in the classroom.

  • MA-EL Master of Arts (Phil) in Educational Leadership
  • MWS-E Master of Worldview Studies in Education
